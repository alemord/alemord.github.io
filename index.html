Something about my hometown:

Florence was founded as a Roman military colony about the 1st century bce, and during its long history it has been a republic, a seat of the duchy of Tuscany, and a capital (1865–70) of Italy.

During the 14th–16th century Florence achieved preeminence in commerce and finance, learning, and especially the arts.

For many, Florence is synonymous with the Renaissance. Indeed, the city boasts a bevy of artistic and architectural wonders from that time period.

However, Florence’s history doesn’t begin and end with the likes of Michelangelo, Machiavelli, or the Medici
